Let's talk Learning Adventures.

They're short, self-directed projects exploring possible futures for online learning.

And we want to help you start one. It can be simple or complex, a solo or collaborative effort. A piece of original research. A web app or prototype. A study group. Or another scenario you imagine.

What can you explore?

Anything relating to the future of learning. Not totally sure? Here's some inspiration →→→

A protocol for livestreamed study sessions on a topic.

A smart contract system for citation management

An incentive mechanism for accountability of learning goals.

Improving accessibility to knowledge.

How do you adventure?

    • 1
    • Decide what you'd like to explore & how you'd like to explore it. Not sure? Our community can help.
    • 2
    • Once you've begun, you start recording your references, ideas & notes publicly so others can follow in your footsteps.
    • 3
    • When you feel your Adventure is complete, you create and publish an artifact that can educate others about what you've discovered. It could be in the form of a blog post, photo essay, lecture, or anything you can think of.

Adventurers in Residence

We're here to support everyone who wishes to embark upon a Learning Adventure. This includes access to tooling, on-going feedback, and participation to the Playground community. Our AIR program extends this to include a $5,000 grant. Everyone is welcome to apply. To learn how we assess & award AIR adventures, click here.
